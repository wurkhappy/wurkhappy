Getting Started

How much does it cost to join?
Nothing! Wurk Happy is free to join!

How long does it take to sign up?
It only takes a few minutes create a Wurk Happy account.

What information do I need to sign up?
Wurk Happy requires your name, email address, and telephone number. In order to receive payments, you will need to set up an Amazon Payments business account. Amazon needs additional personal information to verify your identity.

Why do I need to sign up for an Amazon Payments account?
Contractors who want to collect payment from their clients need an Amazon Payments account to receive payment. We believe Amazon provides the right mix of reliability, security, and simplicity our customers need in order to feel confident sending and receiving funds online.

How do I sign up for an Amazon Payments account?
Go to the Payment Information tab in your account settings and click the "Accept Marketplace Fee" button to get started with Amazon. You will be guided through Amazon's sign up process and you'll have to verify your email address and bank account information.

Wurk Happy charges a 3% fee for each transaction. There are no monthly charges.
