Introduction

This Privacy Policy is incorporated by reference into the Wurk Happy Terms of Use. The term “Wurk Happy,” “we,” and “us” includes Wurk Happy Inc. and our affiliates. The Privacy Policy explains how Wurk Happy may:

  • collect,
  • use, and
  • disclose

information it obtains through the “Wurk Happy Service.”

The “Wurk Happy Service” is defined as any website, mobile application, or Internet service under the control of Wurk Happy Inc., whether partial or otherwise, in connection with providing an online platform for Wurk Happy’s services.

Policy Scope. This Privacy Policy covers how we treat personal information (“Personal Information”) and other information that we collect through the Wurk Happy Service.

Personal Information Defined. We consider “Personal Information” to include information that alone or when in combination with other information may be used to readily identify, contact, or locate you, such as: name, address, email address, or phone number. We do not consider Personal Information to include information that has been anonymized so that it does not allow a third party to easily identify a specific individual.

Use and Acceptance. By using this website or the Wurk Happy Service and accepting the Terms of Use, you also agree to this Privacy Policy. If you do not accept this Privacy Policy, you must not use this website or the Wurk Happy Service.

New Uses of Personal Information and Other Information. From time to time, we may use Personal Information for new, unanticipated uses not previously disclosed in our Privacy Policy. If our information practices change regarding information previously collected, we will make reasonable efforts to provide notice and obtain consent to any such uses as may be required by law.

Posting of Revised Privacy Policy. We will post any adjustments to the Privacy Policy on this web page. The revised version will be effective at the time it is posted. If you are concerned about how your information is used, bookmark this page and read the Privacy Policy periodically.

Other Websites and Pages. When you navigate away from the Wurk Happy Service to websites or apps controlled by third parties, you leave the Wurk Happy Service, at which point this Privacy Policy no longer applies. You will not receive a notice warning when you leave the Wurk Happy Service.

THE WURK HAPPY SERVICE COLLECTS YOUR INFORMATION

We may collect Personal Information when you:

  • register to use the Wurk Happy Service
  • post work agreements and proposals; and
  • communicate with us.

We also collect information, such as anonymous usage statistics, by using cookies, server logs, and other similar technology as you use the Wurk Happy Service.

We do not collect the Personal Information you submit to make a payment.

Personal Information Collection. You must register to use the Wurk Happy Service. To register, you may need to provide Personal Information, such as your name, username, password, email address, and telephone number. You may also provide other optional information.

Work Agreements and Proposals. We may also collect Personal Information provided in the work agreements and proposals you post on the Wurk Happy Service.

Customer Support. We may collect Personal Information through your communications with our customer-support team.

Making Payments. When you pay a freelancer through the Wurk Happy Service, you may need to provide Personal Information to our third-party service providers. We do not collect any of the Personal Information you provide to make a payment, but we may collect information about the payment, such as the date and amount of the payment.

Cookies, Automatic Data Collection, and Related Technologies. The Wurk Happy Service collects and stores information that is generated automatically as you use it, including your preferences and anonymous usage statistics.

When you visit the Wurk Happy Service, we and our third-party service providers receive and record information on our server logs from your browser, including your IP address, and from cookies and similar technology. Cookies are small text files placed in visitors’ computer browsers to store their preferences. Most browsers allow you to block and delete cookies. However, if you do that, the Wurk Happy Service may not work properly.

By using the Wurk Happy Service, you are authorizing us to gather, parse, and retain data related to the provision of the Wurk Happy Service.

Marketing. We do not rent, sell, or share Personal Information about you with other people or nonaffiliated companies for their direct marketing purposes, unless we have your permission.
