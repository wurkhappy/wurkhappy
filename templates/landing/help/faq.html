What is Wurk Happy?

Wurk Happy is an online platform that facilitates simple agreement, billing, and payment for freelancers and their clients.

We make the payment process fast and transparent by improving communication, reducing administration, and decreasing payment processing times.

Should I Use Wurk Happy?

Yes. You deserve better, easier-to-learn, easier-to-use agreement, billing, and payment software.

How will Wurk Happy Save Me Time and Money?

Wurk Happy is one of the simplest and fastest software services that helps you create and manage agreements, keep clients updated, and get paid.

Wurk Happy makes the following easy:

  1. Creating a cloud-based, professional agreement within minutes
  2. Staying organized as payment happens
  3. Using the best payment processing options available to get paid and pay by clicking a button

Sign Up

How much does it cost to sign up?
Nothing! Wurk Happy is free to sign-up and explore. We only charge a fee to the user receiving payment.

How long does it take to sign up?
It only takes a few minutes to create a Wurk Happy account. However, to start receiving payments online, a user will need to register with Zipmark or Amazon. Registration with these services typically takes 5-15 minutes.

For Freelancers

Creating an agreement

  1. To create an agreement make sure you are logged in to Wurk Happy and are on the “Agreements with Clients” screen. In the top right hand corner of the screen you will see a green “create agreement button”. Press this button and you will be walked through the agreement creation process.
  2. In order to create an agreement you will need to be registered with one of the recommended Wurk Happy payment services. At a minimum you will need to enter the recipient of the agreement’s email address, a description of the services to be rendered, dates of payment within a payment schedule, and those payment amounts.

Requesting payment

  1. When you create an agreement in Wurk Happy, the amount of the agreement is broken down into payment phases. Wurk Happy allows the freelancer to request payment for one payment phase at a time.
  2. In order to request payment you need to be logged into Wurk Happy and be on the agreement screen of the agreement you want to request payment for.
  3. Your agreement must be in the “work in progress stage”. When you hit “mark phase complete and request payment”, Your client will receive an email asking them to pay the current phase of the agreement.

Getting paid
The client will make a payment by clicking on the link in the Wurk Happy email asking them to make payment or just be signing into Wurk Happy and clicking on the agreement in the “Make Payment” stage.

If using Zipmark, the freelancer should receive funds in their bank account by the morning of the next business day. If using amazon, funds should be available within 1 business day in the freelancer’s Amazon account.

For Clients

Requesting an agreement
The client can request an agreement by logging into Wurk Happy and making sure she is on the “agreements with freelancers” screen. The client can request an agreement by clicking on the green “request an agreement” button.

Monitoring Work Progress
The client can monitor Wurk Progress on any of their agreements at any time by logging into Wurk Happy and clicking on the “agreements with freelancers” screen.

Making a payment
The client can make a payment by clicking on the link in the Wurk Happy email asking them to make payment or just be signing into Wurk Happy and clicking on the agreement in the “Make Payment” stage.

Payment Options

Through Wurk Happy you can choose to get paid through Zipmark or Amazon. In order to use either payment option you must register a merchant account with the service of your choice.

Zipmark offers the ability to pay and get paid through online digital check.

  1. Smooth, quick registration for freelancers
  2. Fast payment: Funds become available in the bank account of your choice the morning of the next business day.
  3. Low-Cost; Fees to use this service through Wurk Happy are 1.5% up to maximum fee of $15.
  4. Large Transactions: no limits on transaction size

Registering a Merchant Account with Zipmark

How can I sign up for Zipmark?
You can register for Zipmark here.

How is Zipmark different from other payment services?
Zipmark transactions are just like a check from your account, except without the paper or the trip to the bank for the depositor. What this means is that just like a check from your bank, with Zipmark you pay with your money and get paid directly in your account except without fees or delays.

Some internet and mobile payment tools require you to “load” a payment account just to pay with your own money, and then charge fees and take 24 to 48 hours to get access to money paid. With Zipmark it is always your money in your bank account.

What do I need to send or receive money with Zipmark?
Zipmark users must have:

  1. A checking account at a bank, credit union or thrift, which is in good standing.
  2. A Zipmark account registered at www.zipmark.com (invitation only for now)

When will I get money I received with Zipmark
Most Zipmark transactions are available on the morning of the next business day and are processed through the US Federal Reserve Bank Services. On occasion it can get there even faster.

What will I see in my bank account when I send money with Zipmark
Zipmark payers will see the transaction in their online banking, personal finance site (Mint, Quicken, etc.) or even at the ATM. The record of the transaction will look and feel like you wrote a check from your account and will be accompanied by a check image that indicates who you paid through Zipmark. You can print the check and use it as a record of the transaction if you wish.

How is the information I give Zipmark secured?
Zipmark uses electronic checks that are a financial industry standard and a safe and reliable way of processing payments. It uses technology that has been developed and tested to process your check information securely. Zipmark treats all user information with the same level of security and we never share any of your information with anyone.

How can I contact Zipmark support?
Registered users, please email us and we will respond within 1 hour: concierge@zipmark.com

Everyone else: hello@zipmark.com

Registering a Merchant Account with Amazon

How do I sign up for an Amazon Payments account?
Go to the Payment Information tab in your account settings and click the “Accept Marketplace Fee” button to get started with Amazon. You will be guided through Amazon's sign up process and you'll have to verify your email address and bank account information.

You'll be prompted automatically to sign up for an account when you create your first agreement. If you already have an Amazon.com account, we will set up a Personal Account with Amazon Payments that allows you to send payments using credit cards. There is no separate registration process.

If you want to receive payments using Amazon Payments, or you want to pay using a bank account, you can enhance your Personal Account. There are a several account types to choose from. Learn more about the account types available.

How long does it take to sign up for amazon payments?
On average it takes users about 15 minutes to sign up for Amazon payments. If you already have an amazon account than it can take as little as 3-5 minutes, including verifying a bank account.

What payment methods can I use?
You can make a payment using credit cards, your bank accounts, or Amazon Payments account balance. Accepted credit cards include Visa, MasterCard, Discover, American Express, Diners Club, and JCB. To make a payment using your bank account, you'll need to complete a bank account verification process through your Amazon Payments account.

Am I protected using Amazon payments?
If there are ever unauthorized Amazon Payments charges against your credit card or bank account, you can dispute the charge with Amazon. Depending on how quickly you notify Amazon about any unauthorized transactions, you might not be held responsible for those charges. If you submit payment for a product or service and the service is materially different than agreed upon, you can dispute the transaction and request reimbursement. Learn more about transaction disputes.

How do I withdraw funds from my Amazon Payments Account?
If you want to withdraw funds from your Amazon payments account, you can do so by initiating either an Amazon.com Gift Certificate purchase or a transfer to your bank account. To transfer funds to your bank, you must complete the bank account verification process. Learn more about withdrawing funds from your Amazon Payments Account.
