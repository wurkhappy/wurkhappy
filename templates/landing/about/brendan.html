Brendan Berg Co-Founder & CTO

Brendan has over five years of experience creating web and mobile applications. Before joining Wurk Happy, he designed APIs and built cloud infrastructure as Chief Software Architect of Socialbomb. He has built mobile applications for clients ranging from Technicolor to the online radio startup Jelli. He holds a BA in Computer Science and Sculpture from Bard College, where he focused on self-organizing systems, programming languages, and computer graphics.
